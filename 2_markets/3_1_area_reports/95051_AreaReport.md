# ZIP Code 95051 — Santa Clara, California
*Santa Clara County | West of San José | Central Silicon Valley Core*

## 1. Geological & Physical Setting

### 1.1 Geographic Position
ZIP **95051** covers a large portion of **Santa Clara** on the valley floor, generally positioned between **Sunnyvale** to the northwest and **San José** to the southeast, with strong connectivity to the US‑101 / I‑280 / SR‑85 network and major employment nodes across Silicon Valley.

### 1.2 Natural Boundaries & Topography
- Predominantly **flat alluvial valley terrain** (former orchard/agricultural land).
- Drainage corridors and engineered channels are present, but the area is not defined by steep hillside terrain like the Lamorinda or Peninsula foothill ZIPs.
- Site-specific issues typically relate more to **soil behavior (clay / expansive tendencies)** and **older tract grading** than to slope instability.

### 1.3 Land Use Patterns
- Primarily **residential**, with substantial pockets of **office/R&D/light industrial** consistent with Santa Clara’s Silicon Valley employment base.
- Commercial corridors and neighborhood retail are commonly located along major arterials (e.g., El Camino Real, Lawrence Expressway, San Tomas Expressway, Stevens Creek Blvd adjacency).
- Development pattern is largely **built-out**, with incremental infill, ADUs, and selective redevelopment.

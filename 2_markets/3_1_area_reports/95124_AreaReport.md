# ZIP Code Report: 95124 (Cambrian Area, San Jose)

## 1. Geological & Physical Setting
### 1.1 Geographic Position
ZIP code **95124** covers much of the **Cambrian** area of San Jose, on the city’s south‑central/western side between Willow Glen, Los Gatos, and Almaden Valley. It lies on the Santa Clara Valley floor with gentle slopes and easy access to Highway 17, Highway 85, and Camden/Union corridors.

### 1.2 Natural Boundaries & Topography
The topography is **gently rolling to flat suburban valley floor**, with elevation changes far more modest than Almaden or Los Gatos foothills. Natural boundaries are formed mainly by **Los Gatos Creek and smaller drainage channels**, plus the transition toward hillier terrain southwest toward Los Gatos.

### 1.3 Land Use Patterns
95124 is **predominantly low‑density residential**, with:
- Large areas of **single‑family homes** on small to medium lots.
- Scattered **townhome and apartment clusters**, often near major roads.
- Neighborhood‑serving commercial strips along **Camden Ave, Union Ave, Bascom Ave, and Hillsdale/Branham**.
There is very little industrial land; most residents commute to other parts of Silicon Valley.

## 2. Climate & Environmental Hazards
### 2.1 Local Climate & Weather Patterns
Climate is classic **South Bay Mediterranean**:
- Warm, dry summers; mild, wet winters.
- Temperatures slightly cooler than Almaden but warmer than Peninsula/coastal zones.
- Air quality generally **Good–Moderate**, but sensitive to regional wildfire smoke.

### 2.2 Wildfire Exposure & Historical Fire Activity
95124 is largely **built‑out urban/suburban** with limited direct wildland interface:
- Direct wildfire risk is lower than foothill ZIPs (e.g., 95120) but
- Residents still experience **smoke and AQI degradation** during regional fire events.

### 2.3 Flood, Stormwater, and Water‑Related Hazards
The area is not known for major riverine flooding, but:
- Parcels near **Los Gatos Creek and local channels** may sit within stormwater/flood‑prone zones.
- Heavy winter storms can generate **localized street flooding and saturated soils**.

### 2.4 Seismic Activity & Fault Proximity
As part of the broader Bay Area, 95124 is subject to **regional seismic risk** from nearby faults (San Andreas, Calaveras, Hayward systems). Housing stock is mostly **wood‑frame** construction, which generally performs better than unreinforced masonry, but seismic retrofit status is parcel‑specific.

### 2.5 Environmental Compliance, Superfund Sites & Pollution Indicators
There are **no major Superfund sites** within 95124. Main environmental issues are:
- **Traffic‑related emissions** along major corridors.
- Region‑wide **wildfire smoke** episodes.
Overall, Cambrian is perceived as a **clean, low‑pollution suburban environment** by Bay Area standards.

## 5. Commute & Access
### 5.1 Major Roadways, Transit Access & Walkability
Key arteries:
- **Highway 85** along the south edge; **Highway 17** to the west for access toward Campbell/Los Gatos and 280/880.
- Major surface routes: **Camden, Union, Bascom, Leigh, Hillsdale, Branham**.
- Walkability is **neighborhood‑scale** (walk to parks, local schools, nearby shops); broader errands and commuting are car‑centric.
Public transit:
- Served by **VTA bus lines**; no BART or Caltrain station inside 95124 (nearest Caltrain stops in **Capitol, Tamien, Campbell, or downtown SJ/Los Gatos corridors**).

### 5.2 Typical Commute Times to Key Employment Areas
Approximate one‑way drive times in normal (non‑peak) traffic:
- **Downtown San Jose / Diridon:** ~15–25 minutes.
- **North San Jose (Cisco, airport area):** ~20–30 minutes.
- **Cupertino / Apple Park:** ~20–30 minutes via 85.
- **Mountain View / Google:** ~25–35 minutes via 85 → 237 or 85 → 280.

Peak‑hour commutes can push the upper end by **10–20+ minutes**, especially northbound mornings and southbound evenings.

### 5.3 Infrastructure Constraints & Planned Improvements
- The main constraint is **dependency on congested freeways (85, 17)**.
- City and VTA projects mostly focus on **corridor improvements, signal timing, and transit enhancements**; no immediate game‑changing rail project is planned within 95124.
